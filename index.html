About Us


Victory Jolicage Academy in Trans-Nzoia County is a vision born out of the desire to provide a serious learning environment to children with a unique approach to their growth and development. Parents want quality education for their children; they want improved grades for their children, discipline, and a pathway towards professional success. Besides, we strongly focus our teaching on the current government-established competency-based curriculum (CBC).

Leadership and Governance

 The vision bearers of Victory Academy are individuals with experience in Early Childhood Education (ECE), international exposure and a broad understanding of the utility of education. Their knowledge and experience will be utilized to ensure that the school develops into a haven of learning and a centre of excellence not only in Trans-Nzoia County but also the whole of Kenya. Victory Academy has a 7-member advisory board of management. Their work is majorly centred on quality assurance, oversight, and logistical interventions. The founder who is also the patron of the school, oversees the school’s overall leadership of the school and whole infrastructure set-up and development of the school i.e., expansion. The founders who are strongly pro-better education for our children strongly believe that having a high-quality school is critical to the successful development of Kaisagat and the larger Trans-Nzoia County. He believes that education is power. The school has a headmaster who is tasked to ensure smooth learning in the school. There is also a deputy headmaster, who assists the headmaster. Besides the two, we have teachers, a nutrition guide, a school secretary, and a maintenance crew.

Core Operational styles


It is our belief that Victory Academy has a responsibility to provide access to better quality education to all children in the Kaisagat area regardless of gender, tribe, or financial circumstances so that the learners can realize their potential.

Victory Academy will fully utilize the CBC curriculum for children as stipulated by the Ministry of Education as its teaching guide.

We believe that Victory Academy is ideally placed to provide academic solutions to children in the Kaisagat area who are ready to utilize our learning resources towards greater improvement and future academic and professional growth.

We will utilize local personnel as support staff, infrastructure, and land to enhance learning, extracurricular activities, and physical education for the children.

We will develop evidence-based teaching practices that support the achievement of the best possible educational outcomes for our students.

We believe that great dreams are those born out of a mere thought before they are pursued to create a tangible effect to impact humanity.

    Future Plans
  • Victory Jolicage Academy has a future plan of setting up dorms to facilitate residence in the school.
  • We intend to set up a library for the school that will serve the faculty and pupils. The same will have a computer lab with unrestricted online access.
  • In 2 years, the school will review its progress to see if it can start a junior secondary school division.
  • We believe that exploring new horizons is part and parcel of learning. As an institution, the school will pursue partnerships with other local and foreign schools for purposes of information sharing, and academic exchange programs.
  • Victory Jolicage Academy will expand nationally by setting up branches of the same in other parts of Kenya.
  • We shall introduce at least one foreign language teaching module in our readiness to diversify the student’s scoop of language.
